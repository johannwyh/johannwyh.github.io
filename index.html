Biography

My name is spelled 王宇晗 in Chinese.

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University. I am a member of MMLab@NTU, advised by Prof. Chen Change Loy. My current research lies in computer vision, especially video generation and its downstream tasks.

Before joining NTU, I earned my bachelor degree from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University. I had some wonderful research experience in Tencent Youtu Lab with Dr. Ying Tai and Junwei Zhu. I am also an ACMer, participated in six ACM ICPC/CCPC Asia Regional Contest, with four gold medals and two silver medals.
