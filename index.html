Biography

I am Yuhan Wang (王宇晗), currently a Senior Researcher at Hunyuan, Tencent, where I work on 3D generation foundation models for Hunyuan3D. I am also completing my Ph.D. in College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University, affiliated with MMLab@NTU, advised by Prof. Chen Change Loy. My current research interests are in 3D representation and generation.

During my industry research internships, I am honored to have worked with Zeyu Hu, Weikai Chen, and Xin Wang at LightSpeed Studios, and with Ying Tai and Junwei Zhu at Tencent Youtu Lab. Before NTU, I earned my bachelor’s degree from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University. I participated in six ACM ICPC/CCPC Asia Regional Contests, earning four gold medals and two silver medals.

We are hiring! Hunyuan3D is looking for research interns, opportunities in both Singapore and China. Feel free to reach out through email or LinkedIn.
